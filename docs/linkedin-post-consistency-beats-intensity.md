# LinkedIn Post: Why Consistency Beats Intensity in Business

---

In business, intensity gets celebrated.
Consistency gets results.

We admire the all-nighters, the sudden growth spikes, the "worked 18 hours a day" stories. They sound heroic. They look impressive on social media.

But most sustainable businesses are not built in moments of extreme effort. They are built in quiet, repeatable actions done over time.

**Intensity is loud. Consistency is effective.**
